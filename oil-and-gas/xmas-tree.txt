Glossary

Fittings: various components that are used to control the flow of hydrocarbons, facilitate the connection of different pipes or valves, and ensure the overall integrity of the system.
  eg: Master Valve, Wing Valve (válvula lateral), Choke (estrangulador), Swab Valve (valvula de intervenção), Flowline Connection (Conexão da Linha de Fluxo), Crown Plug (tampão superior), Annulus Valve (válvula do anel Anular)...
  ...Pressure Gauge (Manômetro), Injection Port (Porta de Injeção), Casing Hanger (Suspensor de Revestimento), Tubing Hanger (Suspensor de Tubulação), Christmas Tree Cap (Tampa da Árvore de Natal), Safety Valve.
Spool: sections of pipe with flanges on either end connecting different parts and that resemble the shape of a traditional spool used for winding thread.
  ptg: trechos de tubulação. Spool: carretel.
